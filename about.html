Meet the Founder – Manu Maso

Manu Maso is a Computer Science graduate from the University of Papua New Guinea, specializing in full-stack development, UI/UX design, and ICT support services.

He founded PNGTechCo to empower individuals, businesses, and institutions with affordable, modern, and professional digital solutions across Papua New Guinea.

With strong experience in software, hardware, graphic design, and educational ICT support, Manu brings a complete approach to digital transformation in local communities.

Technology should make life easier. My mission is to build solutions that help people work smarter, communicate better, and grow their vision.
